3. Economic Concepts 💡

Opportunity Cost

When you lock cash into a house downpayment, you lose the opportunity to earn 7% annually in the stock market. Our engine calculates this loss exactly!

Sunk Costs vs Equity

Rent is "paying someone else's mortgage", but buying has unrecoverable costs too! Mortgage interest, property taxes, and maintenance are money you never get back.

Compound Interest

By renting and investing the monthly difference, your money makes money. Over 30 years, that snowball effect is mathematically massive.
